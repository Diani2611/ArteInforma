"We are and we make art"


ArteInforma is a technological platform, created with the objective of giving value and recognition to art.

Our mission is to achieve that emerging artists and their works receive recognition and monetize their work; creating a space where art consumers can interact with them, learn about their work, their process and if they wish to purchase their pieces.

As a value proposition we offer artists the possibility of being published for free, not only on our platform, but also to advertise on social networks and a commission cost for the sale of their works lower than other platforms.

We believe in new artists and we work for them.
